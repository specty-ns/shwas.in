Who are we

Education in its purest sense is expanding the horizon of human perception. But we see some of the nation's toughest challenges


Teacher-student ratio
Infrastructure
Cultural background
Poverty

All of these resulting into no pre-primary education, weak academic foundation and maximum dropout ratio at 8th and 10th grade, which forces the students to pursue the labour work their parents are doing.


Shwas,started in 2008, as a solution intervenes by providing opportunities to the less resourceful kids. These kids, registered at a nearby municipality or trust based school, requires support for learning academics.

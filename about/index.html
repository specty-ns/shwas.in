Shwas Learning Centers

The Process

Identifying a community and making parents realise the importance of educating their children.

Renting infrastructure, nearby the communities' dwellings, to provide skills training and education to the children.

Resourcing full time educators to provide education, keeping in mind the ratio of 1 teacher to 5/6 students.

Verticals worked upon

Strengthening academic fundamentals
Awareness about hygiene & nutrition
Skills development
Vocational trainings
